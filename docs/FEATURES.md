# Features

Complete list of features available in Little Boys e-commerce platform.

## 🛍️ E-commerce Features

### Product Management
- **Product Catalog** - Browse products by category (Toys, Clothing, Books, Outdoor)
- **Product Details** - Detailed product pages with multiple images
- **Image Gallery** - Thumbnail navigation with zoom functionality
- **Product Filters** - Filter by category, age range, and special tags
- **Product Search** - Real-time search across products
- **Sort Options** - Sort by price, rating, newest, featured

### Shopping Experience
- **Shopping Cart** - Add, remove, and update quantities
- **Cart Sidebar** - Quick access to cart from any page
- **Wishlist** - Save favorite products for later
- **Size Selection** - Size picker for clothing items
- **Quantity Selector** - Adjust product quantities
- **Stock Indicators** - Real-time stock availability

### Product Information
- **Age Recommendations** - Age-appropriate product suggestions
- **Safety Badges** - Eco-friendly, organic, STEM, safety tested
- **Product Ratings** - Star ratings and review counts
- **Price Display** - Regular and sale prices with discount percentages
- **Category Tags** - Visual category identification

## 📝 Content Features

### Blog System
- **Blog Posts** - Rich content articles with categories
- **Search & Filter** - Search articles by title, author, or content
- **Category Filter** - Filter by Toys, Fashion, Books, Outdoor, Education
- **Sort Options** - Sort by newest, oldest, or title
- **Pagination** - Navigate through multiple pages of articles
- **Related Articles** - Discover similar content
- **Share Functionality** - Share articles on social media

### Product Reviews
- **Write Reviews** - Modal form with star rating
- **Review Display** - Show customer reviews with ratings
- **Verified Purchases** - Badge for verified buyers
- **Helpful Votes** - Mark reviews as helpful
- **Review Sorting** - Sort by date or helpfulness
